Williams-Sonoma Classic Apron, French Blue

A generously sized apron is a necessity in any kitchen, and ours will bright yours with lively color. Sewn of think cotten, it can be personlized or monogrammed with up to nine characters, all the same height, embroidered in your choice of color. An apron of this quality makes a welcome gift for any cook.

  • Durable 100% cotton construction.
  • Adjustable neckband ensures a good fit.
  • Roomy front pockets hold small tools.
  • Machine-wash.
